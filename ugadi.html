Ugadi Celebrations

Come and join with us!!!!!!!!!

The Ugadi event in college is a joyful celebration of the Hindu New Year. The campus is decorated with colorful rangoli and flowers. Students and faculty dress in traditional attire and participate in a puja ceremony. The festivities include cultural performances, such as dance and music, and the preparation of Ugadi Pachadi, a dish symbolizing life's various experiences. Workshops, competitions, and guest speakers add to the excitement, and the event concludes with a community feast. Ugadi in college brings everyone together, fostering cultural appreciation and unity.
